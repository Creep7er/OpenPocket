# PopugVPocket Branding

Product identity is centralized in `app/runtime/branding/brand_config.gd`. The product is **PopugVPocket**, the organization namespace is **Popugonet**, and the release is **0.5.0 Reborn**.

Project-authored pixel assets live in `branding/`; runtime copies live in `app/assets/branding/`. Master marks use transparent backgrounds, hard pixel edges, nearest filtering, and a limited palette. V-Parrot is the system mascot and may represent boot, success, error, installation, achievement, offline, empty, and warning states.

Android uses a two-layer adaptive icon. `branding/android/icon-background.png` is the full-bleed dark background; `branding/android/icon-foreground.png` is a compact transparent V-Parrot emblem. The foreground stays inside the central adaptive-icon safe zone and does not include its own circular or console-shaped container, so circle, squircle, and rounded-square launcher masks do not crop the mark.
